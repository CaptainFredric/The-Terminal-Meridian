FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is the data real-time?

Pro users get streaming live quotes. Free users get 15-minute delayed quotes, the same delay used by most consumer trackers. Both tiers refresh automatically while the panel is open.

Where does the data come from?

Quotes flow through licensed market data providers. News headlines and AI summaries use public news APIs plus GPT and Claude. The whole stack is open source on GitHub if you want to inspect the wiring.

Is my account data private?

Watchlists, positions, alerts, and ticker notes stay scoped to your account. Notes are stored locally first and only sync to your workspace when you're signed in. Email is used for login and account-related notifications only, never sold or shared.

What happens if the server is down?

The terminal degrades gracefully: the last quotes that loaded stay on screen, charts render from cached data, and the UI surfaces a "Server waking up" indicator. The free tier sleeps when idle, so the first visit each day takes ~30 seconds to warm up.

Can I trade through Meridian?

No. Meridian is information-only. We never recommend buying or selling securities, never route orders to a brokerage, and never auto-trade on alerts. You can paper-trade in the Portfolio panel for practice.

Why is it free?

The free tier is genuinely free, forever. Pro and Pro+ subscriptions cover the live data feeds and AI tokens. We don't sell your data, run ads, or rent your watchlist to anyone.
